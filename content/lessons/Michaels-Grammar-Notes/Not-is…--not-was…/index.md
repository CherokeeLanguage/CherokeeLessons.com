Use “Ꮭ” and add the prefix “Ᏹ-”.

  - Ᏹ- + Ꮥ- becomes ᏱᏗ-

Examples:
<!-- more -->
  - Ꮭ ᏱᏥᏁᎦ. “I am not speaking.”

  - Ꮭ ᏱᏁᎦ. “You are not speaking.”

  - Ꮭ ᏱᎧᏁᎦ. “He/she is not speaking.”
