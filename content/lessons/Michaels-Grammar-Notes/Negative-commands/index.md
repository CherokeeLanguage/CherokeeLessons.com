### ᏞᏍᏗ… Ꮵ-

To indicate a negative command:

Use the word “ᏞᏍᏗ (hle²sti)” and add the prefix “Ꮵ- (ji²-)” to the
<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Immediate Tense</span> stem.
<!-- more -->
  - Ꮵ- + Ꮥ- becomes ᏥᏙ-

  - Ꮵ- + Ꮻ- becomes ᏧᏫ-
