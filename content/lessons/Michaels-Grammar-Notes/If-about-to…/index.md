Add the prefix “Ᏹ-” to any of the “Is about to” forms.
<!-- more -->
  - ᏯᎩᏁᏥᏗ. “If I am about to speak.”

  - ᏱᏣᏁᏥᏗ. “If you are about to speak.”

  - ᏳᏁᏥᏗ. “If he/she is about to speak.”
