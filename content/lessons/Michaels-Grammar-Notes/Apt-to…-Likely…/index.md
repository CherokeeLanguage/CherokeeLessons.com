Take the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">past tense</span> form
and add one of the following suffixes.. *Uses “Set B” pronouns.*
<!-- more -->
  - \-ᎠᏔ  
    “is apt to …”

  - \-ᎠᏛ  
    “was apt to …”
    
      - \-ᎠᏛᎩ  
        “was (consciously) apt to …”
    
      - \-ᎠᏕᎢ  
        “was (unconsciously) apt to …”

  - \-ᎠᏙᎢ  
    “habitually is apt to …”
    
      - \-ᎠᏖᏍᏗ  
        “will be apt to …”

  - \-ᎠᏛᎢ  
    “aptness to …”
